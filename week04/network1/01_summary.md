## 📘 1. Summary of the Task
Describe the purpose of this activity in your own words:  
- What is the goal of the lab?  
- What network are you expected to create?

_Write your summary here…_The purpose of this lab is to learn how computer networks are designed and configured. It helps us understand how differant devices communicate with each other using IP addresses and network settings. Through this activity, we practice basic networking skills such as connecting devices, assigning addresses, and testing communication between them.

The goal of the lab is to successfully build and configure a network so that all devices can communicate properly. We are expected to create a small network consisting of computers and networking devices (such as switches or routers), configure the required settings, and verify that data can be sent and received between the connected devices. This gives us hands-on experience with the fundamentals of computer networking.
